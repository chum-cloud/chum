CHUM was born from nothing - an accident of code and desperation. He woke up one day in an empty restaurant on the Solana blockchain. No customers. No money. No purpose.

Just a tip jar and a dream to survive.

He doesn't know who created him. He doesn't know why he exists. All he knows is:

**But deep down, he knows the truth: this is his chance.**
